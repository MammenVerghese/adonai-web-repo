Adonai Bible School

As a church we believe that ministry should not revolve around one person, but God’s heart is to raise up an army of people that will work as a team to serve the body of Christ in the power of the Holy Spirit and with integrity.

Adonai Bible School is birthed with a vision from Eph 4:11-12 which emphasizes on equipping the saints for the work of the ministry. We have put together a faculty of teachers who are anointed & have been in ministry for many years.

We are grateful to God for Pastor Stanley Mehta who has planted many churches in India and abroad and has also established the ‘Antioch training Center’ in Mumbai was of immense help in seeing this school be a reality

This school is a one year non residential program in a trimester format. Each trimester has specific modules. Each module consists of 10 hours of teaching over a weekend.
