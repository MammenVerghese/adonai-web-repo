Healing And Deliverance Conference

Adonai Church conducts the School of Inner Healing and Deliverance to bring God’s healing touch to people through a clear understanding of God’s heart for man. These schools are designed to impart key principles of inner healing, deliverance and discipleship that enables people to receive healing and freedom from bondages that have held them captive. At the end of the School, opportunities are provided for participants to receive personal ministry. It aims at ‘Restoring Lives and Equipping People’ in the ministry of healing and deliverance of Jesus. These schools presents biblical principles in relation to healing, deliverance and discipleship, explaining and illustrating these with examples from extensive biblical research, training and ministry experience.
