Prayer Ministry Seminar

A key part of Adonai Ministries is to be a resource to the local church through their leadership. It is our desire to see people restored and equipped in this ministry of healing and deliverance, from a place of brokenness to a place of wholeness.

This course offers you skills and practical ways in which you can grow in discernment. On completing this seminar a person has the opportunity to join the Adonai Ministry Team to gain a firsthand experience in ministering to people. This ministry gives a message of hope to the hurting and broken-hearted and also provides the opportunity to equip others to minister effectively to those in need. We would be delighted to have you or your leadership team on this training.

Attending the Adonai Healing and Deliverance Conference is a pre-requisite to enroll for this seminar
